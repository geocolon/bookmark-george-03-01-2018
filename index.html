What is Simple Bookmark?

With this web application, you can save a website as bookmarks to a list and add your own descriptions and titles.

Please head to the menu bar at the top of this page and click Make Your Bookmarks.

Once you on the next page and see a form, add your desired title of the bookmark. Next, copy and paste the websites URL you are saving. Then, use the rating system drop-down menu to rate the value of the bookmark. The last input field is to add your own descriptions about the bookmark.

In the saved bookmark area there are two buttons MORE INFO and DELETE.
